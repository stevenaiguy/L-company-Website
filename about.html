About Lixel

Freeing spatial intelligence from 2D screens.

Lixel develops glasses-free 3D floating display and mid-air interaction systems. We bridge the gap between human understanding and complex 3D data, making spatial communication faster, clearer, and far more intuitive.

Turning 3D information into intuitive experience

At Lixel, we believe complex spatial information should be easier to see, explain, and interact with. By combining display innovation with intuitive interaction, we create new ways for people to communicate and understand 3D content in real space.

Liberating Spatial Data

In professional fields, vital information is spatial by nature. Viewing a 3D organ or mechanical part on a 2D screen forces the brain to mentally reconstruct geometry, reducing efficiency and clarity.

??

Unobstructed Vision

Lixel breaks structural data out of the screen. By creating glasses-free floating visuals, we allow users to interact with and dissect 3D complexity naturally, as if holding the object in their hands.

??

Immediate Consensus

Whether it's a surgeon explaining a procedure or engineers analyzing a drone path, removing the visual barrier makes communication immediate, learning deeply engaging, and collaboration frictionless.

Vision

The future is shared spatial decision-making.

We believe the future of communication must move beyond 2D screens. As information complexity grows and AI becomes deeply entangled in how we work, there is a profound need for more intuitive ways to share spatial context.

Lixel is building the interface where humans and intelligent systems can collaborate, understand, and make critical decisions together, effortlessly, around the exact same 3D content in shared space.

Company Profile

We are driven by a singular belief: technology must reduce the cognitive load required to understand complex spatial structures.
