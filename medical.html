Empowering Medical Communication

Lixel provides glasses-free 3D floating visuals for medical education, doctor-patient communication, and advanced presurgical planning.

Medical Education

Help students build stronger anatomical understanding through interactive 3D floating visuals created directly from real imaging scan data and slice integration.

Lixel gives educators a much more intuitive way to teach spatial relationships compared to flat texts, all while allowing them to preserve valuable, rare specimen-based teaching materials in a safe, reusable digital form.

Doctor-Patient Communication

Use patient-specific scans and turn them into floating holograms to explain complex conditions, treatment plans, and necessary procedures in a clearer, more visual way.

By replacing intimidating flat medical sketches or static 2D slice images with dynamic 3D interactive content, Lixel ensures patients and their families understand critical medical information with much greater confidence and trust in their physician.

Radiation Therapy Eval

Evaluate target volumetric models and precise radiation dose distributions seamlessly in 3D mid-air. Lixel enables highly precise pre-operation visual tracking so oncology teams can thoroughly communicate impact fields.

Advanced Pre-operative Communication

Bring high-stakes situations like amputations, reconstructive surgeries, and major orthopedic procedures into 3D. Allow surgeons and patients to align completely on the exact extent of physical intervention before entering the OR.
